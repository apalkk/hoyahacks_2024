        Admission Representatives
      








      WE'RE HERE FOR YOU
    





Your admission representative can answer your questions, offer advice and guide you through the admission process. We understand selecting a college is a big decision, but it doesn’t have to be a stressful one. Learn more about each of our reps, and their territories, by clicking on their photo.
To get in touch with your admission representative, simply email ApplyMaryland@umd.edu and include your city and state in the subject line (for example, "Student Inquiry from College Park, Maryland").
