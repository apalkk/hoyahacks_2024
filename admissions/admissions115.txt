German Studies











Undergraduate students can pursue a B.A. or Minor in German Studies or a double degree in Engineering and German.

The University also offers special opportunities to:

Develop German fluency through the on-campus Language House Living & Learning Residential Program
Deepen linguistic and cultural knowledge through Study Abroad
Practice German with a native speaker on-campus through the SLLC Language Partner Program
Enhance studies through the German Studies Honors Program














Major in German Studies
Develop advanced language proficiency in German and demonstrate knowledge of the institutions, values and cultural products of the German-speaking world. Explore the German Studies B.A.




Minor in German Studies
This 15-credit minor provides students with an in-depth understanding of German language, literature and culture. Discover the minor in German Studies. 




Double Major in Engineering and German
The Engineering and German Program (EGP) promote technological, linguistic, and cultural competence for students interested in both engineering and German. Explore the Engineering and German Program.
